Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Ballooncini

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm AEST). For urgent matters, call us directly at (02) 9716 7600 for immediate assistance.

Is the quote really free?

Yes, absolutely! Our initial quote is completely free with no obligation. We'll discuss your event vision, answer your questions, and provide pricing at no cost. There's no pressure to commit.

What should I prepare for the consultation?

Have a general idea of your event date, venue, colour scheme, and budget range. If you have inspiration photos from Pinterest or Instagram, those are really helpful but not required. We'll guide you through the rest.

Do you work with venues outside the Inner West?

Yes! While we're based in Haberfield in Sydney's Inner West, we provide balloon decoration services across all of Greater Sydney, from the Northern Beaches to Sutherland Shire and everywhere in between.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most events, and 6-8 weeks for weddings and large corporate functions. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.

Can I see examples of your work?

Absolutely! Check out our gallery on the homepage or follow us on Instagram for our latest creations. We're happy to share examples relevant to your event type during your consultation.

Do you offer payment plans?

For larger events like weddings and corporate functions, we offer a deposit and balance payment structure. We'll discuss payment terms that work for you during the quoting process.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

We need your event date, venue location, type of event, any colour scheme or theme preferences, and your approximate budget. The more details you provide, the more accurate our quote will be.
