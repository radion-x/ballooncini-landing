Invisalign FAQs

Everything you need to know about Invisalign clear aligner treatment

How long does Invisalign treatment take?

Treatment time varies based on case complexity, but most patients complete their Invisalign journey in 12-18 months. Simple cases may take as little as 6 months, while more complex cases can take up to 24 months. During your free consultation, we'll provide a personalized treatment timeline based on your specific needs.

Am I a good candidate for Invisalign?

Invisalign is suitable for most teens and adults who want to straighten their teeth. It can treat crowded teeth, gaps, overbites, underbites, crossbites, and open bites. The best way to find out if you're a candidate is to book a free consultation where we'll assess your teeth and discuss your goals.

Does Invisalign hurt?

Most patients experience mild pressure when they start wearing new aligners, which is a sign that treatment is working. This discomfort typically lasts only a day or two and is much less than traditional braces. There are no metal brackets or wires to cause mouth irritation.

How often do I need to wear my aligners?

For best results, you should wear your Invisalign aligners 20-22 hours per day. You can remove them for eating, drinking (except water), brushing, and flossing. The more consistently you wear them, the faster you'll see results.

Can I eat and drink with Invisalign?

You should remove your aligners when eating or drinking anything except water. This means no food restrictions—eat whatever you like! Just remember to brush your teeth before putting your aligners back in to maintain good oral hygiene.

How much does Invisalign cost?

Treatment costs range from $6,500 to $9,000 depending on case complexity. We offer flexible payment plans starting from just $90 per week with 0% interest options. Health fund rebates are accepted, and some patients may be eligible for Medicare Child Dental Benefits. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans to make Invisalign affordable for everyone. Options include weekly or monthly payments with 0% interest financing available. We accept all major health funds and can help you maximize your rebates. Discuss payment options during your free consultation.

What's the difference between Invisalign and braces?

Invisalign aligners are nearly invisible, removable, and more comfortable than traditional metal braces. There are no dietary restrictions, easier oral hygiene, fewer office visits, and no metal brackets or wires. However, both options are effective—we'll help you choose the best treatment for your needs.

Is Invisalign covered by health insurance?

Most private health insurance plans with orthodontic coverage will provide rebates for Invisalign treatment, similar to traditional braces. We accept all major health funds and can help you understand your coverage. Bring your health fund details to your consultation.

What happens after my treatment is complete?

Once you've achieved your perfect smile, we'll provide retainers to maintain your results. You'll need to wear retainers as directed (typically at night) to prevent your teeth from shifting back. We include retainers in your treatment package and offer follow-up appointments to ensure lasting results.
