Balloon Decoration FAQs

Everything you need to know about our balloon decoration services

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for standard events, and 3-4 weeks for large or complex installations. However, we do accommodate last-minute requests when possible — give us a call and we'll do our best!

What types of events do you decorate?

We decorate for all occasions — birthday parties, anniversaries, weddings, corporate events, product launches, grand openings, school events, baby showers, and more. No event is too big or too small!

Do you deliver and set up?

Yes! We offer delivery and professional setup across all of Greater Sydney. Our team arrives early to ensure everything is perfect before your event begins. Delivery fees vary based on location.

Can I choose custom colours and themes?

Absolutely! We specialise in custom designs. Share your colour palette, theme, or inspiration photos and we'll create balloon decorations that match your vision perfectly.

How long do balloon decorations last?

Air-filled balloons typically last several days to a week. Helium-filled balloons last 8-12 hours for standard latex and up to 2-3 days for foil balloons. We time our setups to ensure maximum impact during your event.

Do you offer helium balloons?

Yes! We provide helium gas balloons, floating bouquets, and helium-filled arrangements. We also offer air-filled options that last longer and are more eco-friendly for certain installations.

Are your balloons eco-friendly?

We're committed to sustainability. We use top-tier, biodegradable latex balloons and eco-friendly materials wherever possible. We also offer reusable balloon options for environmentally conscious clients.

Can you do corporate or commercial installations?

Yes! We have extensive experience with corporate events, mall installations, retail promotions, and commercial displays. We can work with your brand colours and incorporate logos into balloon designs.

Do you offer printed balloons?

Yes, we offer custom printed balloons with your text, logos, or designs. These are perfect for corporate branding, promotional events, and personalised celebrations. Contact us for minimum order details.

How do I get a quote?

Simply call us at (02) 9716 7600, fill out our enquiry form, or chat with us online. Tell us about your event — date, venue, guest count, and theme — and we'll provide a custom quote within 24 hours.
